Services

About Uncle Kev

I've been a petrol head my whole life. What started as an informal apprenticeship in my old man's garage where I would do nothing more than hold the torch, turned into a love for diagnosing problems and fixing them, no matter how challenging.

"Call uncle Kev". Whenever anyone close to me had a problem with their car, it was always "call uncle Kev and ask him to look at it" and I loved every phone call and the challenge that came with it. From replacing the throttle body on my son's car, to a quick service fo the old lady down the road, I have always been excited to throw my overalls on and get my hands dirty.
